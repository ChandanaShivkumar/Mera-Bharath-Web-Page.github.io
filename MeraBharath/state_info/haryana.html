HARYANA

It is a state in eastern India. It is the third-largest state by population and twelfth-largest by territory, with an area of 94,163 km2 (36,357 sq mi). It is contiguous with Uttar Pradesh to its west, Nepal to the north, the northern part of West Bengal to the east, and with Jharkhand to the south.

LANGUAGES

Telugu is the official language of Andhra Pradesh, which is also the mother tongue of nearly 90% of the population.

CUISINES

The cuisines of Andhra Pradesh boast of the Nawabi style of the royalty. They use spices, fruits, and vegetable that is grown in the region.

COSTUMES

Women generally wear Saree with a blouse while some Muslim women wear Salwar Kameez. The younger generation can be seen wearing Chudidaars, Pants, Shorts while the older generation wears saree.Men wear short dhoti which is folded about the knee with kurta or shirt.

FESTIVALS

Brahmotsavam, the Hindu festival is an auspicious festival celebrated every year at the Tirumala Venkateswara Temple at Tirupati in Andhra Pradesh.
